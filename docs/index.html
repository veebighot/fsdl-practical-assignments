Hey! I'm Vaishnavi Bighot, a third-year Computer Science Engineering student at PCCOE, Pune. I'm the kind of person who finds herself equally excited about writing a Python script and designing a social media campaign — because I believe the best tech products are built by people who understand both the how and the why.

My primary passions are Digital Marketing and UI/UX Design. I love understanding how people think, what grabs their attention, and how to design experiences — digital or otherwise — that are intuitive, engaging, and meaningful. I've already had the chance to work on real awareness campaigns and design portals that serve actual communities.

On the technical side, I have a solid foundation in C, Java, Python, DBMS, and SQL, and I'm currently working my way through Full Stack Web Development. I want to be the kind of engineer who can design a user journey, code it up, and market it — all in one.

Outside academics, I care deeply about social impact. Two of my projects — a pet adoption portal and a sexual health awareness campaign — reflect my belief that technology should always work toward something bigger.
